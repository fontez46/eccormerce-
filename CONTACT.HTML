Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number on our website's order tracking page to see the current status and location of your package.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be in their original condition with tags attached. To initiate a return, please visit our Returns Center in your account dashboard.

How do I change or cancel my order?

If your order hasn't shipped yet, you can make changes or cancel it by contacting our customer support team through live chat or by phone. For orders that have already shipped, you'll need to wait until you receive the items and then initiate a return if needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa,bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.
